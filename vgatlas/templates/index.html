What is VGAtlas?

VGAtlas is curated, comprehensive, and reproducible video game data bank. Ever needed to check some values, find some image, or cite some text? No longer you're using google images or listening to potato let's players, just look it up on vgatlas.

VGAtlas is in very early stages.
